About Thy Word Media

Thy Word Media is a community showcase for independent Christian content creators. Browse our growing collection of books across a wide range of genres and age groups — from picture books to young adult fiction and beyond.

Every book in this gallery has been submitted directly by its author, so you can discover new voices and stories that you won't always find on the bestseller lists. Use the search and filters on the Browse Books page to find titles by age range or category.

This website is minimally curated.

Readers & Reviewers

Please review and rate content on the relevant platform (Amazon, Goodreads, Substack, YouTube, etc.).

If you find any material that is controversial or inappropriate, please contact the site admin and it will be removed.
