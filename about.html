About Thy Word Media

Thy Word Media is a community showcase for independent Christian content creators in fellowship in New Testament assemblies. — Bible Chapel, Gospel Hall, or similar. Browse our growing collection of books across a wide range of genres and age groups — devotionals, Bible studies, fiction, children's picture books, gospel/evangelistic, apologetics etc..

Every book in this gallery has been submitted directly by its author, so you can discover new voices and stories that you won't always find on the bestseller lists. Use the search and filters on the Books page to find titles by age range or category.

This website is minimally curated.

Readers & Reviewers

Authors: Submit Your Book

We are looking for encouraging and uplifting content. Please note:

  • Controversial or inappropriate content will be removed.
  • This is a showcase, not a platform for advocacy. Please do not submit books whose primary purpose is to argue for a particular theology, agenda, or point of view — even within Christianity, there is a wide range of opinion on many topics, and the site is not in a position to endorse any of them.
  • For now we are only supporting English-language content.
  • You must have the right to promote this material — i.e. you are the content creator, own the rights, or have made a significant contribution to the work.
